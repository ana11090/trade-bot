#### **GROUP R — Ichimoku Cloud** (5 indicators)

Trend following and reversal indicator
- `psar` - Parabolic Stop and Reverse value
- `psar_signal` - Is price above SAR? (1/0)

#### **GROUP T — VWAP** (2 indicators)
Volume Weighted Average Price - critical for day trading
- `vwap` - VWAP value
- `vwap_distance` - Distance from VWAP (%)

#### **GROUP U — Supertrend** (2 indicators)
Popular modern trend indicator
- `supertrend_upper` - Upper band
- `supertrend_lower` - Lower band

#### **GROUP V — Pivot Points** (7 indicators)
Classic support/resistance levels
- `pivot_point` - Central pivot
- `resistance_1`, `resistance_2`, `resistance_3` - R1, R2, R3
- `support_1`, `support_2`, `support_3` - S1, S2, S3

#### **GROUP W — DMI Components** (2 indicators)
Directional Movement components (complements ADX)
- `plus_di` - Positive Directional Indicator (+DI)
- `minus_di` - Negative Directional Indicator (-DI)

#### **GROUP AC — KST** (2 indicators)
